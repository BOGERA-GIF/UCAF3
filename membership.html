Purpose, Mission & Vision

Purpose: To promote and developing paddling sports in Uganda in the spirit of the Olympic Movement
Mission: We are a people focused organisation committed to providing opportunities to everyone in Uganda to paddle.
Vision: To develop the Sport of Canoeing in Uganda, in the spirit of the Olympic Movement.

Learn More

Strategic Objectives

Build strong Coaches and officiating officials base and network through training and capacity building programmes
Improve regional training centre.
Increase participation in paddle sports.
Improve governance and accountability to Members.
